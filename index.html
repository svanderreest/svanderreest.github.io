Travel advice

The church and reception venue are both in Central London, not far from each other. Have a look at Google Maps below.

Walk

It's a 21 minute walk from All Souls to the Royal Automobile Club, 89 Pall Mall.

Tube

All Souls is a five minute walk from Oxford Circus tube station. From there, you can take the Victoria line to Green Park of the Bakerloo line to Piccadilly Circus, which is a seven minute walk from the RAC.

Taxi cab

If you prefer not to walk or take the tube, there are black cabs just outside the Langham hotel across from church.
